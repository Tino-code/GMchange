Ghana Make A Change

At Ghana Make a Change, our mission runs deep: to catalyze lasting change in Ghana's communities. With wells for clean water, support for schools, clinics, and businesses, we're committed to transforming lives.

What We Do

Education

Build and renovate schools to create safe and conducive learning environments and supply educational materials, books, and technology to enhance learning experiences.

Health

Offer medical services and healthcare programs to ensure community well-being. Promote health education and preventive care initiatives.

Nutrition

Assist families with access to nutritious food to combat hunger and malnutrition and also educate communities on healthy eating habits and nutrition.

Businesses

Support the development of small businesses to create employment opportunities.Facilitate market access and business networking to help small enterprises thrive.

About Ghana Make a Change

Welcome to Ghana Make a Change, where passion meets purpose. Founded with a vision to ignite positive change, we are dedicated to transforming lives and fostering sustainable development across Ghana.
Through our unwavering commitment to initiatives in clean water, education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, we strive to make a tangible difference in communities. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future for all.

Donation of Medical Equipments

Ghana Make a Change donates funds and medical equipments to a community in Kasoa. These donations ensured that the local clinics is better equipped to provide quality medical care and address critical health needs.

Trip to Tamale

Our trip to Tamale and the Northern Region of Ghana was a delightful adventure filled with cultural experiences and natural beauty. We connected with the local community in Bolgatanga, learning how to make beehives with twigs and cow dung, and enjoyed an exciting safari in Tamale, where we saw a variety of wildlife, including walking alongside large wild elephants.

Cape Coast Castle

Our trip to Cape Coast Castle was a deeply moving experience, offering a poignant glimpse into Ghana's history. As we walked through the ancient halls and dungeons, we felt the weight of the past and the significance of this historic site. The guides shared powerful stories about the transatlantic slave trade, and we reflected on the resilience and strength of those who endured unimaginable hardships.

Kakum National Park

Our visit to Kakum National Park was an exhilarating adventure immersed in the heart of Ghana's tropical rainforest. We navigated the famous canopy walkway, suspended high above the forest floor, offering breathtaking views of the lush greenery and diverse wildlife below. Walking among the treetops, we spotted various bird species and listened to the sounds of the jungle, feeling a profound connection to nature.
