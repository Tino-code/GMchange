Youth Trips

For two week you will drill wells, help the continuing construction of schools and feed village children. These trips change the lives of so many youth, and they can change yours as well.

Your payment takes care of the total cost of the entire trip. This includes your round trip plane ticket form New York City to Ghana Africa, food & housing, and every other expense you will have during your stay. This fee will not include personal expenses outside of the trip such as souvenirs or other personalized items.

Humanitarian Trip

Embark on a transformative journey to Ghana, where for two weeks, you'll immerse yourself in a variety of meaningful activities. From drilling wells to aiding in school construction, distributing clothing donations, teaching dental hygiene, and nourishing village children, each endeavor promises to touch lives and create cherished memories.

Your participation in these projects not only uplifts local communities but also offers you the opportunity to forge deep connections with the people you meet along the way. With all expenses covered, including round-trip airfare from New York City, accommodations, and meals, this journey beckons you to embrace the beauty of giving back while experiencing the richness of Ghanaian culture.
